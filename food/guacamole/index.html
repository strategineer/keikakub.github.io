Elf's Blood Guac

  • dip
Let the blood of your puny enemies flow while you enjoy this creamy guacamole.
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados (ripe)
  • 1 tbsp of cumin powder
  • ¬º cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 shallot (diced)
  • 1 tbsp of hot sauce (like Frank's RedHot or Cholula)

Steps

  1. Scoop out the insides of the avocados, leaving the seeds behind and put them in a serving bowl.
  2. Mix all the other ingredients in the bowl and squash everything until it's smooth.
  3. Serve with tortilla chips, in quesadillas/tacos/burritos or in sandwiches.
