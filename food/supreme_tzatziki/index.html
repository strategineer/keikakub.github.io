Supreme Tzatziki

Previous Next
Unleash you inner greek god by enjoying this perfect tzatziki.
Serves 12

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cucumber (grated, less seeds is good)
  • 750g of 5% greek yogurt
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 tbsp of fresh dill (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1-12 cloves of garlic (diced)
  • 1 tsp of salt

Steps:

  1. Take one handful of grated cucumber at a time and squeeze it over the sink to remove excess water.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Set aside, covered, in refridgerator for 8h to let the flavors mix.
  4. Serve with bread.

Notes:

  • If you self identify as a basic person then use 1 clove of garlic, if you self identify as a hairy smelly greek man then use up to 12 cloves of garlic.
