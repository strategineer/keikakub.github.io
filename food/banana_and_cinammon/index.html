Sinnamonana

  • dessert
Simple yet delicious sliced banana sprinkled with cinnamon served with honey.
Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Peel the banana.
  2. Slice it into 1cm thick disks on a small plate.
  3. Lay the slices out flat and sprinkle them with cinnamon.
  4. Pour honey onto plate beside the slices or on top of them as desired.
