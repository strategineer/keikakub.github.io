The PCs talk through and create their Voices' skills (GM must approve all skills), adding them to the spreadsheet.

The PCs run the command below to set their Level 1, 2 and 3 Obsessions (Each PC must secretly send their obsessions to the GM for approval beforehand , some private back-and-forth may occur to make them just right).

  • Obsessions are the insane (and sometimes nonsensical) drives of each Voice. Completing them rewards points.
  • Each level of obsession should be progressively harder to achieve.
  • Each should be relatively simple and written in a short sentence.
1
2

Play begins.

How to Play

Telling Secrets

Use the following command to whisper secrets to the GM.

1

/whisper secret: I just achieved my obsession level 3, wake up you cunt

Willpower

  • Each Voice starts with 10 (or 7 if they've chosen 3 skills) willpower
  • Voices spend willpower in 2 ways:
    • In Tests for Control, to assert their dominance and take control of John.
    • To improve the chances of their Skill Checks succeeding
1

/view willpower

Skill Checks

  • Roll a six-sided die:
    • Succeed on a 3 or better for skills you have
    • Succeed on a 6 for skills you don't have
  • Before the roll, you can spend Willpower to add a +1 to the result
1

/roll

or

1

/roll willpower: 2

Tests for Control

Tests for control occur when:

  • John wakes up. (Yesâ€¦ this also means at the start of the session.)
  • John is injured.
  • Any Obsession is completed (by any Voice).
  • Any Check is failed.
  • John Gets Distracted and Falls Asleep (pg. 19).

Each Voice runs the following command with their bid in Willpower points

1

/set bid willpower: 1

The Voice with the highest bid takes control of John. If multiple Voices tie for the highest bid, then dice are rolled to see who becomes the active Voice.

The Voice who becomes active loses the amount of Willpower they bid. All others keep their bids.

Winning

You win by achieving as many of your obsessions as possible. Achieving a level 1 obsession rewards you 1 point, a level 2 obsession 2 points and a level 3 obsession 3 points.

An obsession can be completed at any time, whether or not your voice is currently in control.

Whenever any voice achieves an obsession, a test for control occurs. The GM should usually be aware of when this happens and start a test for control at that point but feel free to secretly nudge him if he forgets (there's a lot of obsessions for him to keep track of).
