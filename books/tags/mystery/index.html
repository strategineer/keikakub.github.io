Murder on the Orient Express by Agatha Christie

Date read: Wed, Nov 29, 2023
  • fiction
  • mystery
Murder on the Orient Express is similar to Agatha Christie’s Death on the Nile (the other Agatha Christie novel I’ve read recently). Both novels involve Hercule Poirot on vacation traveling (on a train or on a boat respectively), with the soon-to-be murder victim asking Poirot for help, him declining and then after the murder occurs, Poirot proceeds to solve the murder. But Death on the Nile was SO MUCH MORE of a compelling read for me.
