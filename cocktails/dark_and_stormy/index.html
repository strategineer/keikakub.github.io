Dark and Stormy

  • ⚖️
  • rum
Dark rum combined with stormy (?) ginger beer. A storm of flavors... In your mouth.
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 204)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Havana Club 7 Years rum
  • 3 oz Fever Tree ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge, for garnish

Steps

  1. Build in an ice-filled highball glass. Add the garnish.

Legend

Build
Fill the glass with ice, if called for, and add the ingredients in the order given. Add any garnish called for, stir briefly, and serve.

Notes

  • Substite 1 oz of Havana Club 7 Years rum with 1 oz of Havana Club 3 Years rum for a Light and Stormy (I haven’t tried this yet but I plan to).
