Dark and Stormy

  • 2021
  • light
  • rum
Previous Next
Dark rum combined with stormy (?) ginger beer. A storm of flavors... In your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz of Havana Club 7 Years rum
  • 3 oz of Fever Tree ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Build (pour each ingredient one after the other into the glass) in an ice-filled highball glass. Add the garnish.

Notes:

  • Substite 1 oz of Havana Club 7 Years rum with 1 oz of Havana Club 3 Years rum for a _Light and Stormy_ (I haven't tried this yet but I plan to).
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 204)
