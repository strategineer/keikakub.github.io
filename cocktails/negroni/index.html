Negroni

  • Strong

  • Gin

Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 254)

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Tanqueray gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Stock Lionello (sweet vermouth)
  • 1 orange twist, for garnish

Steps

  1. Build in any order in an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Add the garnish.

Legend

Build
Fill the glass with ice, if called for, and add the ingredients in the order given. Add any garnish called for, stir briefly, and serve.
