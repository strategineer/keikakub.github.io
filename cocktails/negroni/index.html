Negroni

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz of Tanqueray gin
  • 1 oz of Campari
  • 1 oz of Stock Lionello (sweet vermouth)
  • 1 orange twist, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Build in any order in an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Add the garnish.
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 254)
