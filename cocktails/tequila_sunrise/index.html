Tequila Sunrise
  • Strong
  • Tequila
Inspired by: https://www.saq.com/en/tequila-sunrise-ec

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz. golden tequila
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • 1/4 oz grenadine syrup
  • 1 orange slice, for garnish
  • 1 mint leaf, for garnish

Steps

  1. Shake and strain the tequila and orange juice into an old-fashioned glass with a big ice cube in it.
  2. Pour the grenadine syrup into the orange juice.
  3. Add garnishes.

Legend

Shake and Strain
Fill a shaker two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Shake for approximately 15s and strain the drink into the appropriate glass.
