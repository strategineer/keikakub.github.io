Moscow Mule

  • ⚖️
  • rum
TBD
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 253)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 3 oz Fever Tree ginger beer
  • 2 lime wedges, for garnish

Steps

  1. Build in an ice-filled highball glass. Add the garnish.

Legend

Build
Fill the glass with ice, if called for, and add the ingredients in the order given. Add any garnish called for, stir briefly, and serve.

Notes

  • You can replace the 3 oz of ginger beer with a mix of ginger syrup and club soda to make the cocktail have a little less kick. I haven’t tried this yet so I’m not exactly sure on the ratios.
