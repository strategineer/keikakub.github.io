Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bombay Sapphire gin
  • 1 oz orgeat syrup
  • 1 lime
  • 1/2 oz mint syrup

Steps

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lime.
  2. Shake and strain all ingredients into an old fashioned glass with some ice cubes.

Legend

Shake and Strain
Fill a shaker two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Shake for approximately 15s and strain the drink into the appropriate glass.
