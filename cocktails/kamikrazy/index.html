Kamikrazy

  • 💪
  • vodka
A variation on the "Kamikaze" cocktail. I'm not too crazy about it. Be warned, it's very strong.
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 228)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz blue curaçao
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass

Legend

Shake and Strain
Fill a shaker two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Shake for approximately 15s and strain the drink into the appropriate glass.
