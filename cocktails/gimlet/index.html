Gimlet
    • Strong
    • Gin
    Inspired by: https://jeffreymorgenthaler.com/lime-cordial/

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Tanqueray gin
    • 1 oz homemade lime juice cordial
    • 1 lime wedge, for garnish

    Steps

    1. Stir and strain into an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Add the garnish.

    Legend

    Stir and Strain
    Fill a mixing glass two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Stir for approximately 30s, then strain the drink into the appropriate glass.

    Notes

    • You can make your own homemade lime juice cordial with this recipe
    • Make sure to only zest the green skin of the limes. You don’t want any of the white stuff in your cordial.
