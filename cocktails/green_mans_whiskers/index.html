Green Man's Whiskers
  • Strong
  • Gin
Inspired by: The Joy of Mixology by Gary Reagan (p. 274)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 oz Tanqueray gin
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • 1/2 oz sweet vermouth
  • 1/2 oz fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 oz blue curaçao
  • 1 to 2 dashes of orange bitters

Steps

  1. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Legend

Shake and Strain
Fill a shaker two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Shake for approximately 15s and strain the drink into the appropriate glass.

Notes

  • This is a variation on the “curled” Satan’s Whiskers cocktail which replaces the orange curaçao with blue curaçao because that’s what I had at the time.
