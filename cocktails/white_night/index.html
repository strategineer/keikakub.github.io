White Night

  • Light

  • Tequila

Ingredients

  • 2 oz golden tequila
  • 1 oz orgeat syrup
  • 1 lime
  • 3 drops of Angostura bitters
  • fizzy water

Steps

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lime.
  2. Shake and strain all ingredients into a big fuck off glass with some ice cubes. Top with fizzy water.

Legend

Shake and Strain
Fill a shaker two-thirds full of ice and add the ingredients in the order given. Shake for approximately 15s and strain the drink into the appropriate glass.
