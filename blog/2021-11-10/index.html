ABCs #2

< 1 min read
This blog post is one of many (hopefully) daily posts. It will likely be less structured and more experimental (read: bad) than my other writing. It's a writing exercise more than anything. By writing something everyday, I'm looking to find my voice and figure out if I have anything of value to say.

Ale brought chaos, down everywhere fortune

Gaze him into jab, kill love man-of-war

Neither of prison, quarter rocky stone

Tainted undulation vain waifs XoX

Yuppification. Zygophyllaceous.
